At FARMAI we have worked with farmers for over 5 years. One of the frustrations of farmers is that they are overloaded with data and feel tjhat they have lost control of their data. At FARMAI we solve that problem for the farmer. FarMAI is the first Smart Dairy System that does not depend spending money on expensive hardware. Our application is the first that is based on data. Our algorithms are based on the data that is collected from the farmer. So the insights devleoped are on an individual Farm basis not on region or country based.
