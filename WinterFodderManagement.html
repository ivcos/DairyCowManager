Winter Fodder Budget Calculator

This tool will help you to calculate the amount of fodder you will need for the winter period based on the number of cattle being hosued over the winter. It will also help you to calculate the amount of fodder you will need to purchase to make up any shortfall.

Winter Fodder Budget Calculation Assumptions

  • Assumes 0.175 tonnes of silage 1-2 Year Old per week
  • Assumes 0.325 tonnes of silage per 2+Year per week
